# Session: Partnership Documents Rewrite
**Date:** February 24, 2026
**Focus:** Complete rewrite of all partnership documents based on Michael & Scott's financial advisor feedback

## Executive Summary

Gavin met with financial advisors Michael Hartzman and Scott on Feb 23. They reviewed the original partnership proposal with James (Bullseye founder) and gave strong feedback: the 49% partnership structure (25-30% equity + $8-10K/mo compensation) was too rushed, too risky, and gave away too much. All 6 partnership documents were completely rewritten to reflect the new approach.

## Key Decisions

1. **Equity**: Reduced from 25-30% to 10-20% for $30-50K purchase price
2. **Compensation**: Eliminated entirely. No monthly payments from James. Equity sale provides Gavin's runway.
3. **Timeline**: Extended from 30-day trial to 6-month beta evaluation period
4. **IP**: ALL IP stays with WNU LLC. No co-ownership, no joint filing, no contribution to partnership entity.
5. **Tone**: Shifted from emotional/desperate ("hard pill", "asking as a favor", "96 Bulls") to confident/business-focused ("this is what it's worth, here's the price")
6. **Due Diligence**: Added full checklist (credit check, references, background, business financials) before any binding agreement
