### 7. Advisory Session -- Michael Hartzman & Scott
**Date:** February 23, 2026, 11:49 PM
**Participants:** Gavin, Scott, Michael Hartzman (Scott's father)

Critical advisory meeting. Michael provided experienced partnership guidance.

**Michael's Key Advice:**
- **Too soon for partnership** -- only met 2 weeks ago, one 3-hour in-person meeting
- Gavin has significantly more at stake as sole founder with baby coming
- Partnership is like marriage -- requires years of getting to know each other
- Michael's own successful partnership with Dominic took 4 years of knowing each other first
- Need to understand: ethics, morals, financial alignment, work styles, worst days

**Red Flags Identified:**
- James frequently travels (Italy for a month, often in Europe skiing)
- Employees left his Philadelphia operation
- Different financial situations -- Gavin is broke, James is traveling internationally

**Recommended Alternative Deal Structures:**
1. **Sell 10-20% equity for $30-50k** -- provides 6 months runway without giving away control; include option for James to buy more later
2. **Beta partnership model** -- James beta tests product on his 1,000+ local businesses; either pay James for beta testing OR he pays for small equity stake
3. **Revenue sharing** -- James proposed 10% commission on each successful appraisal; need to calculate actual dollar potential

**Critical Guidance:**
- "Never justify why you need to get paid" -- don't mention baby, health insurance, or financial pressures
- Due diligence required: credit history, business ethics, past failures/successes
- Reference checks with mutual contacts
- Consider siloed revenue model (like Michael & Dominic's partnership)

The formal decision meeting. All three parties on the call. James had just arrived in Italy (Newark to Zurich to Florence). **They agreed on Path 1 -- the 30-day Beta.**

**Deal Structure Agreed:**
- **30-day beta trial** starting March 1, 2026

- James to provide developer access to Shopify
- API costs: ~$0.0025/appraisal (negligible)
- **Hide the rationale/methodology** from public view -- show offer only, not how it works (IP protection)
- Bot protection needed to prevent algorithm reverse-engineering
- Consider whitelisting to James's customer list initially
- First month: bring-to-store only. Shipping/checkout infrastructure is month 2+ scope

**Time Commitments:**
- Gavin: 50% RealWorth (2-3 days/week, Fri/Sat/Sun/Mon), 50% job hunting
- Graham: 10+ hours/week, weeknights best for meetings
- James: TBD after settling in Italy, weeknights for working sessions, mornings for solo brainstorming
