## STAR NOTES

Star replacement notes have a star symbol in serial number:
- Pre-1981: Star at END of serial
- 1981+: Star at BEGINNING of serial
- Premium: $5 - $500+ depending on rarity

## 2013B DUPLICATE ERROR

Modern $1 bills with DUPLICATE serial numbers exist:
- Series: 2013B
- Fort Worth printing
- Matching pair value: $20,000 - $150,000
